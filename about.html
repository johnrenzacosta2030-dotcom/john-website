Our Story

Crafting Timeless Pieces Since 2004

Founded by Gorjana Reidel, our brand was born from a love of travel and a passion for simple, beautiful jewelry that women can wear every day. What started as a small collection of friendship bracelets has grown into a beloved jewelry line sold in our own boutiques and fine retailers worldwide.

Each piece in our collection is thoughtfully designed and handcrafted with attention to detail and quality. We believe in jewelry that becomes part of your personal story - pieces you'll reach for every day and want to pass down to the next generation.

Commitment to ethical sourcing and environmental responsibility is at the heart of everything we do. All our materials are carefully selected to meet our high standards for quality and sustainability.
